Introduction

Our mission is to offer you all the necessary tools to manage your gym.

We keep all your data safe in the cloud. No more lost of information.

You can access to your manager from everywhere. You just need an internet connection.

Features

Manage

A more easily and better way to manage your client and payment.

Inform

Send a message to your clients at any time by mail, SMS, whatsapp...

Schedule

Handle the gym schedule easily. Add training sessions and different events in few clicks.

Stats

Get a lot of kind of stats about your gym like, new clients per week, how many clients has come, the peak of attendance.

Close to the gym

Keep your client close to the gym thanks to extra features and our smartphone application.

Business

Keep a record of all your client payments.

Music

Let your client manage the music in the gym when they are here! More fun!
